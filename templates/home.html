Who are we?

The Antigravity Racing Foundation is a passionate community of people that love the WipEout video game series. We began public operation on March 2nd, 2024, when we started inviting people to our Discord server.

The community was co-founded by ThatOneBonk and ChaCheeChoo. The former handles community affairs, promotion, various graphics, miscellaneous programming and formal writing; the latter is the talented developer who made Thallium+Beat.

The point of AGRF was to unify the people and create a place where Thallium+Beat players could organize and meet, but it exceeded that original purpose, thanks to the countless people who have contributed to AGRF and to keeping the game alive. These people organized in-game and real life events, gave feedback, inspired, played, talked and welcomed AGRF's way into being the WipEout community to be in.

  • We're a passion project.
  • Some of our software is open-source.
  • Our API endpoints for Thallium+Beat - player list, lobby list and leaderboards - are available to developers on request.
  • We have a press kit.
  • If you're as passionate about WipEout as we are, you should join.
