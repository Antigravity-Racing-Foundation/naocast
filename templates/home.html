Welcome to the official Antigravity Racing Foundation Website!
Our flagship project, Thallium+Beat, allows you to play WipEout HD and WipEout Pulse online again, as well as re-experience most of WipEout 2048's online features. We also support MotorStorm: Arctic Edge!
Except for WipEout 2048 Online Campaign and some WipEout Pulse in-game website features, everything works!
We hold several weekly events on our WipEout Discord server, including:


WipEout HD Weekly Lobby, a time to simply play with others, on Saturday at 19:00 UTC;


WipEout HD Mode Shuffle, an appreciation of often neglected ways to enjoy the game every Sunday at 19:00 UTC;


WipEout Pulse Portable Rush, its version of Weekly Lobby, held every Thursday at 19:00 UTC.
